WHO ARE WE?

ArenaPlay is a blockchain-based social gaming platform. We intend to provide multiple sustainable revenue streams to incentivize gamers to turn their passion into a career.

HOW WE DO IT?

We aim to make gaming its own sustainable ecosystem via tokenization
ArenaPlay is an ERC20 token ArenaPlay is an ERC20 token that used on the platform which holds real-life value.

ERC20 Token

ArenaPlay is an ERC20 token. The Ethereum platform was developed by Vitalik Buterin to create a platform that fully took advantage of the blockchain technology.

Token Rewards

Skilled players will be able to accumulate tokens in the form of tournament winnings among other activities. Our platform offers a pathway to becoming a professional gamer with token rewards.

Speedy and Anonymity

Massive token winnings on our blockchain-based platform will be executed with speed and anonymity.

Player versus Player

Tournaments

Tournament hosted on ArenaBet’s platform will be like other skill-based types, the main difference being that the reward settlement for the winning player or team will be based on a smart contract that is tied to a tournament bracket on the platform instead of in-game events.
